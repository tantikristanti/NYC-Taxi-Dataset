# NYC Taxi Dataset

NYC taxi dataset is a comprehensive dataset containing millions of rows of taxi trip information, published monthly by the New York City Taxi and Limousine Commission (TLC).

**Releases**
* Yellow trip data (2024-2026): https://github.com/tantikristanti/NYC-Taxi/releases/tag/v1.0.0-yellow-alpha
* Green trip data (2024-2026): https://github.com/tantikristanti/NYC-Taxi/releases/tag/v1.0.0-green-alpha
* Taxi zones: [https://github.com/tantikristanti/NYC-Taxi-Dataset/releases/download/v1.0.0-zones-alpha](https://github.com/tantikristanti/NYC-Taxi-Dataset/releases/tag/v1.0.0-zones)

**Dataset Contents**

This dataset includes:

* Pickup and drop-off timestamps.
* Geographic locations of pickup and drop-off points.
* Passenger demographics (e.g. solo, group, family trips).
* Trip distances.
* Fare structures (including taxes, tolls, payment methods, rate types, and payment types)

**Data Sourcing**

The data is sourced from authorized technology providers under the Taxicab and Livery Passenger Enhancement Programs (TPEP/LPEP).

**Additional Resources**

For more information on the dataset, please refer to:

* **Taxi Zone Maps and Lookup Tables**: Access to maps and lookup tables for taxi zones in each borough.

**Publication Schedule**

Trip data is published monthly on the TLC website, typically with a two-month delay to allow time for full vendor submissions.

**Format**

The dataset is stored in the PARQUET format. For more information on how to view or export the data, please refer to [NYC Open Data](https://www.nyc.gov/site/tlc/about/tlc-trip-record-data.page).

**Disclaimer**

The datasets are provided "as-is" and may be subject to minor changes in the near future to standardize the PARQUET schema across all years and datasets.
